About Us

Chef Michael has worked in many distinguished fine dining establishments across the country and has held several notable roles that helped shape his approached in providing an elegant, fun and relaxed culinary experience. As a Certified Executive Chef through the American Culinary Federation, a bachelor’s degree in culinary management from the Art Institutes of Austin and a Master’s in Business Administration from Norwich University, Chef Michael is a skilled, professional chef that adheres to the highest food and business standards. By preparing delicious and nutritionally balanced food Chef Michael ensures the service of pleasurable dining experiences.
